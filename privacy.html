Privacy Policy

Effective 23 July 2026. This policy explains how Northeast Basecamp uses information shared through our website.

Information we collect

When you send a booking request or enquiry, we collect the information needed to respond and arrange your stay: your name, phone number, selected package, arrival details, group size, food preference and any notes you choose to provide.

How we use it

We use this information to check availability, confirm booking details, coordinate festival stays, answer questions and provide customer support. We may contact you by phone, WhatsApp or email regarding your request.

Sharing and storage

Booking information is stored in our secured booking system. We share only the necessary details with operational partners involved in your confirmed stay, transport or add-ons. We do not sell personal information.

Your choices

You may ask us to correct or delete your enquiry information, subject to legitimate operational and legal record-keeping needs. Contact us at sales@northeastbasecamp.com.

Updates

We may update this policy as our services evolve. The effective date above will change when we do.
